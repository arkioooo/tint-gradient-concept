So you might have heard about these in articles or your teacher might have mentioned these, but do you really know about Black Holes? Of course not! Or else why would you be here? Before we learn about black holes, let me explain a little bit of a backstory. The universe is filled with giant balls of gas and fusing atoms. When all the atoms get bigger, the star begins to either shed its outer layers or explode in an awesome process called a SuperNova, where the star literally explodes. If there isnt a supernova, then the star sheds its outer layers and slowly becomes a dwarf ( I know! lame, right? ). Anyways, when the star explodes, it becomes a neutron star, or a magnetar, and eventually its core collapses on itself and becomes a Black Hole.
